As students, we often end up eating the same things: junk food, quick meals, or just pasta.
Between limited time, tight budgets, and lack of motivation, it can be difficult to discover and try healthier and more varied food.
That’s exactly why we created MealEarn.
MealEarn is a mobile app designed to help students improve their eating habits in a fun and motivating way.
You simply log or scan your meals, and the app analyzes them to give you a variety score.
Based on your preferences, MealEarn suggests new meals to try, helping you discover healthier options you might actually enjoy.
And the best part: the more varied and balanced your meals are, the more points you earn, which can be exchanged for rewards like vouchers, restaurant discounts, or cooking goodies.
So instead of eating the same thing every day, why not turn healthy eating into a rewarding experience?
Try MealEarn today! Eat better, discover more, and get rewarded.
